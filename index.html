We know Asbestos!

It has been estimated that 3,000+ different products contain asbestos including: attic insulation, hot water insulation, vinyl floor tiles, window caulking, siding material, and many more. Although asbestos is more common in older homes, it can be found anywhere as it is technically not illegal and continues to be used in United States in certain products and applications.

At AMAX, our employees have decades of experience in removing asbestos and know what to look out for. We area able identify quickly potential risk areas, so you can be sure that your home or business is in good hands.
